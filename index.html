I am a PhD candidate in the College of Economics and Finance at Hanyang University.

我是汉阳大学经济与金融学院的博士研究生。

My research asks how taxation, fiscal policy, and life-course shocks shape inequality, intergenerational outcomes, and individual health, labor supply, and well-being; I aim to estimate credible causal effects and to uncover the mechanisms behind them.

我的研究关注税收、财政政策以及人们在生命历程中所经历的冲击,如何塑造不平等、代际结果,以及个人的健康、劳动供给与福祉;我力求估计可信的因果效应,并揭示其背后的作用机制。
